About Us

Welcome to the Anglican Church of the Resurrection Meadowlands, a vibrant, God-centered parish in Zone 7, Soweto, part of the Anglican Church of Southern Africa and the Diocese of Johannesburg. Our diverse community of approximately 1,200 members includes professionals, business leaders, and individuals from various backgrounds. We offer dynamic worship services on Sundays at 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM, with additional services on Wednesdays and Thursdays. Our spiritual life is enriched by a blend of Anglican traditions and cultural expressions, with active lay participation in ministries like Scripture reading and communion assistance. Join us to experience God's presence and connect with our welcoming community. For more information, contact us at 011 536 5191 or [resarec@iburst.co.za](mailto:resarec@iburst.co.za).
